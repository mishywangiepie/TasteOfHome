Hello!

My name is Michelle Wang. I am a second year at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. Find me hammocking on the glade, painting by numbers swaddled in blankets, or binge watching Criminal Minds and Silicon Valley. This site was inspired by my mother's cooking and traditional Taiwanese recipes that have been passed down to me orally since I was a kid, and is my attempt to bring others my taste of home even without her.
